### Transcriptions are often needed in sustainability 
Numerous aspects of sustainability involve conversations:
* *Interviews* are incredibly common across sustainability subfields
* *Seminars*, *panels*, and *conferences* are vital to exchanging knowledge
* *Reels* and *podcasts* are also increasingly common.

Transcribing all these conversations can be a good idea: 
* To *quote* in various formats
* To *read faster* (and repeatedly)
* To *search* for specific sections
* To *anonymise* participants more easily
* To use in the context of *data analytics*
* To multiply your *marketing* content.

However, transcription is incredibly time-consuming, and paid transcriptions can be costly.
